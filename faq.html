Theatre refers to the live performance aspect, with actors, sets, and a stage. Drama is the written script itself.

The atmosphere can vary depending on the type of play. Dress code is usually casual, but check the theatre website for our recommendations. Arrive a bit early to find your seat and settle in. Turn off your phone and enjoy the live performance!

No, typically photography and videography are not allowed during a performance. This is to ensure a distraction-free experience for everyone.

Some plays might be intended for mature audiences, so check if there's an age limit.
