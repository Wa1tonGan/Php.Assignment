Behind the Curtains

Our Prologue

Just as every theater piece begins with a curtain rise, our journey commenced in the quiet town of Street, Somerset, where Cyrus and James Clark embarked on a mission to craft a shoe as captivating as any stage performance. From sheepskin off-cuts to the spotlight of craftsmanship, our legacy parallels the timeless narratives spun in the theater.

Artisans and Innovators

Our inception is akin to the opening act of a groundbreaking play. From the pioneering creation of the slipper by Cyrus and James Clark to the world’s first foot-shaped shoe, our history is a tapestry of innovation and ingenuity. Our designs, inspired by the dramatic and the subtle cues of theater, cater to every performer's needs, ensuring comfort and elegance on and off the stage.

Crafting for the Stage

"A masterpiece of engineering and a work of art," so Leonardo da Vinci described the human foot, an apt analogy for the theater itself. In crafting shoes perfect for life’s performances, we delve deep into the biomechanics of feet in motion, much like a choreographer studies movement, to create footwear that supports every step of your journey, whether under the spotlight or in the wings waiting for your cue.
